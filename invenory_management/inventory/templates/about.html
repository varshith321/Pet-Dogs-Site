About Us

Pups and Teacups is the best place in town to play with pups and grab your favorite coffee or tea beverage. We are dedicated to helping homeless furry friends find their forever homes, so if you fall in love with a furry friend during your visit, please see the counter about adopting them. Our mission is to revolutionize dog adoption by offering a comfortable and fun space for humans and dogs to hang out with each other, away from overcrowded shelters, which can provoke fear and aggression in perfectly adoptable pups. In addition to helping pups find homes, we also offer the opportunity for people unable to have pets of their own to spend quality time with furry friends (and vice versa) without the commitment of adoption.
